University of Maine

To begin my second semester of college, I showed up to The Universtiy of Maine not knowing what to expect. My roommate laughed at me quite a bit due to the stupid questions I would ask. Coming from a college where I wasn't allowed to do much of anything, the thought of being able to do pretty much whatever I wanted was quite foreign to me. As I began my first couple of weeks I didn't care much for UMO, but as time went on, I made friends, and the campus made for a much more enjoyable college experience. Not to mention, I'm able to go home whenever I want now, whereas before I couldn't go home without being $400 dollars poorer, and having a 24 hour travel day. Along with all this, the Army program at the University of Maine was just rated by the United States Army as one of the top ROTC programs in the nation. I knew having come from The Citadel that Maine's ROTC program was better, but never would I have imagined that it was one of the top programs in the nation. All in all, I've begun to enjoy my time at University of Maine Orono, and I can't wait to see what the future has in store for me.
