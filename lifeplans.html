Life Plans

As I mentioned in the, "About Me" section, my life's dream is to become an officer in the United States Army. Along with that I hope to branch infantry and obtain my Ranger Tab, and go on to serve my country to the best of my ability. Although this may sound cliche, it's a big motivater in my life to do what I'm doing each and every day. As former President John F. Kennedy said, "Ask not what your country can do for you, but what you can do for your country." That line from his speech has stuck with me during every difficult challenge I've had to face concerning the military, and that's why I felt led to do what I'm doing. After my cerrer in the Army, I would like to retire and move back to the New England area to either become a police officer, or open up my own little diner. I've enjoyed cooking and baking ever since I was small, and I don't believe it would be a bad job after a stressful career in the Army. Not to mention I'd be back in, "Pat's Nation" with my fellow Patriots.
